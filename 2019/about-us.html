THE ORGANIZER - AFRICA RUBY COMMUNITY
The African Ruby Community (ARC) is a community of 5000+ Ruby Developers developers (Engineers), entrepreneurs, designers and freelancers with chapters in Nairobi- Kenya, Kampala - Uganda, Kigali-Rwanda, and Dar es Salaam- Tanzania.
ABOUT THE CONFERENCE - RUBY CONF AFRICA 2017
On the 25th to 27th July 2019, leading Ruby developers from around the world will come together to share, inspire and learn. RubyConf Kenya is an inclusive conference and as such strives to be welcoming and accessible to as many people as possible.
