THE ORGANIZER - AFRICA RUBY COMMUNITY
The African Ruby Community (ARC) is a community of 5000+ Ruby Developers developers (Engineers), entrepreneurs, designers and freelancers with chapters in Nairobi- Kenya, Kampala - Uganda, Kigali-Rwanda, and Dar es Salaam- Tanzania.
ABOUT THE CONFERENCE - RUBY CONF AFRICA 2016
On the 8th to 9th May 2016, leading Ruby developers from around the world will come together to share, inspire and learn. RubyConf Kenya is an inclusive conference and as such strives to be welcoming and accessible to as many people as possible.

The University of Nairobi is a collegiate research university based in Nairobi and is the largest university in Kenya.

African Nazarene University (ANU) is a hub for technological innovation and education, offering programs in Computer Science, Information Technology, and related fields. With a focus on equipping students with cutting-edge skills, ANU fosters research, innovation, and ethical leadership in the tech industry.
